Short Bio
I am currently a third year PhD student in the School of Mathematics and Computational Science, Sun Yat-sen University, China, advised by Prof. Jianhuang Lai and Dr. Wei-Shi Zheng. I obtained my Bachelor degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2010. My research interests are in computer vision and applied machine learning, including human-object interaction modeling, action recognition, large margin learning and 3D face shape modeling.
